Deakin

Deakin University is a leading public university located in Victoria, Australia, renowned for its commitment to innovative education and research. Established in 1974 and named after Alfred Deakin, the second Prime Minister of Australia, the university has grown into a vibrant community of over 60,000 students, offering a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various disciplines. At Deakin, we prioritize student success and employ a hands-on approach to learning, ensuring that our courses are industry-focused and designed to equip graduates with the skills needed for the modern workforce. Our faculties encompass a wide array of fields, including arts and education, business and law, health, and science and engineering, allowing students to pursue their passions and career goals in a supportive environment.

Deakin is also recognized for its strong emphasis on research, with numerous institutes dedicated to advancing knowledge in areas such as health, sustainability, and technology. Our research initiatives are designed to address real-world challenges and contribute positively to society. With campuses in Melbourne, Geelong, and Warrnambool, as well as international locations in Indonesia and India, Deakin University provides a globally connected education experience. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive community, supporting students from all backgrounds, including a strong focus on Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander education.

Join us at Deakin University to embark on a transformative educational journey that prepares you for a successful future. Whether you are an international student or a local learner, you will find a welcoming environment that encourages growth, innovation, and lifelong learning.
